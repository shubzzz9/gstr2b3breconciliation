GST Reconciliation FAQ

Quick answers to the questions accountants and business owners ask most often about GSTR-2B reconciliation and the free TechBharat tool.

What is GSTR-2B?

GSTR-2B is a static Input Tax Credit statement issued by the GST portal. It lists invoices reported by suppliers and is used while deciding ITC for GSTR-3B.

How often should I reconcile?

Reconcile every month before filing GSTR-3B. Waiting until quarter-end or year-end makes supplier follow-up harder and can delay eligible ITC.

Can I reconcile without uploading my data?

Yes. The free reconciliation tool runs in your browser. Excel parsing and invoice matching happen locally on your device for previewing results.

Does the tool work with Tally Prime exports?

Yes. It supports standard Tally exports and custom purchase register Excel files. Column auto-detection handles common GSTIN, invoice, taxable value, IGST, CGST, and SGST headings.

What file formats are supported?

The tool supports Excel files in .xlsx and .xls formats. Download GSTR-2B as Excel from the GST portal and export Tally purchase data as Excel.

Will it detect GSTIN typos?

Yes. GSTINs appearing on only one side are flagged for review, and similar invoice numbers can be shown as possible matches.
